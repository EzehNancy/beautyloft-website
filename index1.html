Aftercare

Make it last.

A few habits that keep your color, cut, and nails looking fresh between visits.

✂️ Hair

  • Day 1–2Skip washing for 48 hours after color so it fully sets.
  • WashUse a sulfate-free shampoo — sulfates strip color fast.
  • HeatAlways use a heat protectant before hot tools, every time.
  • WaterRinse with cooler water — hot water opens the cuticle and fades color.
  • RefreshBook a gloss or toner every 4–6 weeks to keep balayage from turning brassy.

💅 Nails

  • First hourAvoid hot water or dishwashing right after a gel set while it fully cures.
  • DailyApply cuticle oil every night — it's the single biggest thing for gel longevity.
  • GlovesWear gloves for cleaning or dishes — chemicals lift gel edges fast.
  • Don't peelNever pick or peel gel off — it takes layers of your natural nail with it.
  • TimingRebook fills every 2–3 weeks before lifting turns into breakage.

Good to Know

Frequently asked.

Do you take walk-ins?

Walk-ins are welcome when there's an opening, but booking ahead guarantees your spot — especially on weekends.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

A small deposit is required for color and full nail set appointments to hold your slot. It's applied toward your total at checkout.

How far in advance should I book?

1–2 weeks ahead is ideal, especially for color or special-occasion styling. Weekend slots fill up the fastest.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Inspo photos help a lot — text them ahead of time if you can. For color appointments, come with clean, product-free hair.

What's your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged a fee.
