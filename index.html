Aftercare

Make it last.

A few habits that keep your color, cut, and nails looking fresh between visits.

Hair

Skip washing for 48 hours after color so it fully sets.

Use a sulfate-free shampoo — sulfates strip color fast.

Always use a heat protectant before hot tools.

Nails

Apply cuticle oil every night — it's the biggest thing for gel longevity.

Wear gloves for cleaning or dishes — chemicals lift gel edges fast.

Never pick or peel gel off — it takes layers of your natural nail with it.
